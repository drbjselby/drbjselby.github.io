About Me

Hello!

My name is Bekah Selby and I am an assistant professor of economics at Emporia State University. I teach a wide variety of economics and applied econometrics courses at both the graduate and undergraduate levels. I was instrumental in the development and launch of the Masters of Science in Informatics - Quantitative Economics program at ESU and I serve as the lead advisor for this program. I also serve as the Associate Director of the Centers for Community Research and Economic Education. I am active in my community and passionate about helping make changes to our world to improve it for our future generations.
